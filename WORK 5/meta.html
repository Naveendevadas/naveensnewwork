Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

preperation time:30 minutes

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cup Chocolate Chip

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree f (175 degree C).
  2. In a large bowl,cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Bear in the eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. In a seperate bowl, combine the flour and baking soda; gradually add to the butter mixture.
  5. Stir in the Chocolate Chip
  6. Drope by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
